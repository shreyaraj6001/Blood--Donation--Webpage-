Give Blood, Save Lives

🩸 What Is Blood Donation?

Blood donation is a voluntary act of giving your blood so it can be used to help people who are sick, injured, or undergoing medical treatments like surgery, cancer therapy, or trauma care. One donation can save up to three lives.

🩸 Why Donate Blood?

Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Your small act can save up to three lives.

🩸 Why Blood Donation Is Important

  • Saving Lives:
    Blood is needed for:

  • Accidents and trauma

  • Surgeries

  • Cancer treatment

  • Anemia

  • Hemophilia and clotting disorders

  • Complications in childbirth

  • Organ transplantation
