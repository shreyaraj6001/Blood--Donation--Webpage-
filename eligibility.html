Who Can Donate Blood?

  • Age between 18 and 65

  • Minimum weight of 50kg

  • Good health condition

  • No major medical issues

  • Hemoglobin Level:
    Men: at least 13.0 g/dL
    Women: at least 12.5 g/dL

  • Recent flu, cold, fever → wait until fully recovered

  • Recent surgery → wait period varies

  • Pregnancy → usually wait 6 months postpartum

  • Recent dental work → 24–72 hours depending on procedure
